## Bug Fixes

- Average and Median values in the room round summary are now rounded to at most 2 decimal places instead of displaying the full floating-point value (e.g. `8.88888888`).

- The issue queue now shows the correct number of issues per page right after a Jira import. Previously the user who ran the import saw only 5 issues per page regardless of available screen space and had to refresh the page to fix it — the page-size measurement was poisoned while the import panel temporarily replaced the queue list in the layout. Queue and history page sizes now re-measure reliably whenever the list reappears.

## Improvements

- Rate limiting added to all API endpoints and WebSocket messages to protect against abusive or runaway clients. Room actions are capped at 20 requests per 10 seconds, Jira operations at 5 per 30 seconds, and login attempts at 10 per 60 seconds. Requests that exceed a limit receive `429 Too Many Requests`.
